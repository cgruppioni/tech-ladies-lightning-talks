Applying to jobs that you feel unqualified for

Interview

  • Ask what the interview questions will be beforehand.
  • Pre-interview coding challenges determine if you are technically fit for the role, in-person whiteboard challenges demonstrate if you are able to communicate and collaborate in a team.
  • They WANT to hire you.
  • Ask a lot of questions to show them how interested you are in the company/role.

"Do you have any hesitations about me as a candidate?"
